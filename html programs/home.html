PARAGRAPH TAG :

      Paragraphs can contain many different kinds of information. A paragraph could contain a hello this is span tag of brief examples or a single long illustration of a general point. It might describe a place, character, or process; narrate a series of events; compare or contrast two or more things; classify items into categories; or describe causes and effects. Regardless of the kind of information they contain, all paragraphs share certain characteristics. One of the most important of these is a topic sentence.
